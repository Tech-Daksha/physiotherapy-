Journey of Roshan Raul

Welcome to Roshan Raul's journey as a Physiotherapist in Kandivali, where healing and wellness converge to transform lives. Founded with a vision to provide exceptional physiotherapy care, my journey began with a commitment to serving individuals suffering from injuries, sports-related ailments, senior citizens seeking rejuvenation, and those recovering from accidents or damage. Over the years, I have embarked on a mission to redefine rehabilitation by offering personalised, holistic treatments that prioritise the well-being and recovery of every patient who walks through our doors.
